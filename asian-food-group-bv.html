Category Opportunity Brief, South Indian and Kerala Ambient Food

Current Range Analysis

Your portfolio reflects a deliberately built sourcing strategy across East and Southeast Asia, with over 380 brands spanning Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, China, Japan, South Korea, and Malaysia. Within Indian food specifically, your range centres on TRS, covering the pan-Indian pantry staples of spices, lentils, rice, and flours. It is a strong foundation for that segment, and a clear signal that South Asian sourcing already sits inside your buying remit.

The Commercial Gap
Across 380 brands and 3,500 products, the South Indian and Kerala ambient snack and pickle category is entirely unrepresented, a structurally distinct segment that TRS's North Indian pantry range was never built to cover.
The Minzelis Solution

TRS gives your buyers herbs, spices, rice, and lentils. It does not extend into Kerala-style ambient snacking, banana chips, tapioca chips, traditional mixtures, or the regional pickle formats that sit alongside them on a South Indian shelf. That is a different supplier relationship entirely, built on origin-specific recipes and category depth rather than pantry breadth.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving you an EU-compliant route to capture this category gap without adding sourcing risk to a range you've spent years building carefully.
