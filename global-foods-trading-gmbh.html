Category Opportunity Brief, South Indian and Kerala Ambient Food

Current Range Analysis

GFT has built one of the strongest South Asian supply positions in Europe, anchored by Haldiram's for North Indian snacks, TRS across spices and pulses, and the in-house Schani brand for everyday Indian cooking. The move into South Indian cuisine via Anjappar — Chettinad sauces and cooking products — shows GFT has already identified South Indian as a growth segment worth owning. The 4,000-plus SKU catalogue represents a genuinely comprehensive pan-Asian range, with meaningful exclusive distribution rights across five product verticals.

The Commercial Gap
Anjappar captures Chettinad cooking. The Kerala ambient shelf — banana chips, tapioca chips, jackfruit chips, regional mixtures, and South Indian pickles — remains entirely absent from GFT's 4,000-product portfolio, leaving a daily-purchase, diaspora-driven category uncaptured across five European markets.

Kerala-origin ambient snacks are not a niche. They are the everyday food of a distinct, high-purchasing South Indian diaspora population spread across GFT's UK, Germany, and Netherlands customer base. Unlike Chettinad cooking products, which are occasional purchases, banana chips and Kerala mixtures are weekly staples. No brand in GFT's current range addresses this. The shelf space and the customer demand exist — the supply connection does not.

The Minzelis Solution

Minzelis is a premium South Indian and Kerala ambient food brand, managed from Germany and built specifically for the European retail and wholesale channel. The range covers Kerala snacks, South Indian pickles, pulses, and pantry staples — the categories GFT's current portfolio does not reach.

The brands GFT carries — Haldiram's, TRS, Schani, Anjappar — do not overlap with Minzelis. This is a genuinely additive range, not a substitution. South Indian and North Indian shoppers are largely buying different products. Adding Minzelis does not cannibalise existing GFT volume; it captures a different basket.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving GFT an EU-compliant, documentation-ready route to capture this category across all five of your European markets in one relationship.
