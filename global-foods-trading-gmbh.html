Category Opportunity Brief, South Indian and Kerala Ambient Food

Current Range Analysis

GFT has built one of the most complete South Asian supply positions in Europe, anchored by Haldiram's for North Indian namkeens, TRS across spices and pulses, and the in-house Schani brand for everyday Indian cooking essentials. The expansion into South Indian cuisine via the proprietary Anjappar brand — Chettinad sauces and cooking products — signals that GFT has already made the strategic decision to move beyond North Indian. The 4,000-plus SKU catalogue, distributed exclusively across five European markets, is a genuinely serious range built over four decades.

The Commercial Gap
Anjappar captures Chettinad cooking. The Kerala ambient shelf — banana chips, tapioca chips, jackfruit chips, regional mixtures, and South Indian pickles — remains entirely absent across GFT's 4,000-product portfolio, leaving a high-frequency, diaspora-driven category uncaptured in every one of your five European markets.
The Minzelis Solution

Minzelis is a premium South Indian and Kerala ambient food brand, managed from Germany and purpose-built for the European retail and wholesale channel. The range covers Kerala snacks, South Indian pickles, pulses, and pantry staples — the categories GFT's current portfolio, including Anjappar, does not reach.

The brands GFT carries — Haldiram's, TRS, Schani, Anjappar — do not overlap with Minzelis. This is a genuinely additive range. South Indian and North Indian shoppers buy different things. Adding Minzelis captures a different basket from a different buyer, with no cannibalisation of existing GFT volume.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving GFT an EU-compliant route to capture this category across all five of your European markets in one relationship.
