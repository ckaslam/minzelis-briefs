Category Opportunity Brief, South Indian Ambient Food

Current Range Analysis

Indian Foods Group has built one of the most credible branded Indian food operations in Australia — an anchor distribution relationship with Haldiram's that now moves over 100 million units a year, complemented by ITC's snack formats, Kawan's frozen breads, and Lata's Kitchen's gourmet frozen range. The portfolio reflects a deliberate strategy: own the North Indian and Pan-Indian pantry across both mainstream supermarkets and the ethnic channel, and execute it at scale.

That strategy is clearly working. The Haldiram's partnership culminating in a joint European manufacturing venture is evidence of a business that earns category-defining relationships, not just distribution slots.

The Commercial Gap
Haldiram's, ITC, Mario, and Kawan together give your buyers a comprehensive North Indian ambient and frozen range — but the South Indian ambient snack and pickle shelf remains completely uncaptured, across both supermarkets and ethnic retail.

None of the brands currently in the IFG portfolio addresses South Indian ambient snacks — banana chips, tapioca chips, regional mixtures, jackfruit chips — or South Indian condiments. These are daily-consumption items for a significant and growing segment of the Australian Indian diaspora, sitting adjacent to the category you already own.

The Minzelis Solution

Minzelis is a dedicated South Indian ambient food brand — 105 SKUs across snacks, pickles, pulses, breakfast staples, and condiments, produced to EU food safety standards at source in South India.

The brands you distribute — Haldiram's, ITC, Kawan — do not produce South Indian ambient snacks. There is no overlap and no cannibalisation. Minzelis slots in as a complementary South Indian layer on top of the North Indian range you already own, giving your buyers a complete Indian ambient offering for the first time.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving you a fully documented, audit-ready route to add this category to your portfolio.
