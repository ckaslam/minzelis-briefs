Category Opportunity Brief — South Indian & Kerala Ambient Food
Your Current Range

Jamoona has built a strong South Indian presence across its platform, with Nirapara covering Kerala-style pickles, Telugu and Priya handling the broader South Indian pickle range, and Haldiram's Kerala Mix and Telugu jackfruit chips giving some South Indian snack coverage. Across staples, the Daawat and Aashirvaad ranges are well-stocked, and the platform clearly serves a South Indian diaspora audience across Germany and the Netherlands.

The Gap We Noticed

The South Indian snack range has one notable gap: Kerala ambient snacks beyond banana chips and jackfruit chips. Tapioca chips, pakkavada, regional mixtures from Malabar, Calicut, and Payyoli, and murukku varieties are products with strong repeat-purchase behaviour among Kerala diaspora customers. None of these are currently on the platform under any brand.

On the pickle side, Nirapara covers tender mango and kaduku mango well. But Kerala-specific pickle formats that fall outside the mango category are absent: gooseberry (amla), ginger, garlic, and white lime pickles. These are distinct from the Priya and Mother's Recipe ranges already stocked, and they serve the same South Indian buyer base that Nirapara's customers belong to.

Minzelis closes both of these gaps under one brand, with a range designed for European retail from the ground up.

Why This Category Moves

The Kerala and broader South Indian diaspora in Germany and the Netherlands buys specific snacks, not generic Indian alternatives. A customer who knows tapioca chips from home does not substitute them with potato chips or banana chips. The same applies to pakkavada and to Kerala-style pickles beyond mango. These are distinct products with a loyal, habitual customer base.

The fact that Nirapara is already performing on the platform is the proof point. It confirms that Jamoona's South Indian customers respond to Kerala-branded products and seek them out. What Nirapara covers in pickles, Minzelis can cover in ambient snacks and in the non-mango pickle formats that sit adjacent to Nirapara's range rather than competing with it.

A 105-SKU range from one Kerala-origin supplier, already shipping to 16 countries through Gramarhein, means the supply side is straightforward and the range can be introduced incrementally.

What Minzelis Brings
