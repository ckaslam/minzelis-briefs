Current Range Analysis

M.A. Oriental Foods has built one of the UK's most complete Asian ambient and frozen portfolios, anchored by strong exclusive partnerships. Your Indian section reflects a deliberate strategy, Everest for spices on sole UK distribution, Ashoka for ambient ready meals and condiments, Ching's Secret covering the Indo-Chinese crossover segment, and India Gate for premium Basmati. Across 700-plus SKUs spanning Filipino, Thai, Korean, Japanese, Chinese, and Indian categories, the Indian range is the most structurally developed non-Asian section in your portfolio.

That structure is a commercial asset. It means M.A. Oriental's Indian retail and wholesale customers already rely on you for pantry staples, meal solutions, and spice basics, coming back to the same account for more than a single category.

The Commercial Gap
Across 11 pages of Indian products, not one SKU covers South Indian ambient snacks or Kerala-origin pickles. The customers asking for banana chips, tapioca crisps, and Kerala-style achar are leaving that basket unfilled.
The Minzelis Solution

Ashoka covers North Indian pickles and condiments. Ching's Secret is Indian-origin but specifically Indo-Chinese. Everest is spice-only. None of the brands currently in your Indian range touches South Indian ambient snacking or Kerala-origin pickles at all. That is not a gap that gets filled by expanding any of your existing lines. It is a separate category, with a separate customer demand, that requires a separate branded solution.

Minzelis is that solution. A 105-SKU Kerala-origin range spanning ambient snacks, pickles, pulses, breakfast cereals, chutney powders, and pantry, built specifically for diaspora retail distribution. The format is ambient shelf-stable, the price point is everyday, and the SKUs cover exactly the items South Indian customers currently cannot find in your catalogue.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving you an EU-compliant, well-documented supplier relationship for a category your range is currently missing entirely.
