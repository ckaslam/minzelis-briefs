Category Opportunity Brief, South Indian and Kerala Ambient Food

Current Range Analysis

Your portfolio reflects a deliberate strategy to cover the full breadth of Asian food across Central Europe, with strong representation in Indian spice brands like TRS, MDH, and SHAN, Indian pantry staples through HEERA and PATAK, and a well-developed North Indian snack section anchored by classic namkeens alongside HEERA's papad range. The Indian category is clearly a commercial pillar for your customers, and the depth across sauces, rice, and grocery suggests a customer base that cooks authentically rather than just cooking conveniently.

That same depth makes one absent cluster particularly visible.

The Commercial Gap
Your snack and pickle sections cover North India and Pakistan well. The Kerala and South Indian ambient category, one of the fastest-growing segments among the Indian diaspora in Central Europe, is not represented anywhere in your current 3,500 SKU range.

HEERA's papads and nuts, your North Indian namkeens, and the AHMED and CAMEL pickle lines are doing real work in your portfolio. But none of them speak to the Malayali, Tamil, or broader South Indian customer who is looking for banana chips, tapioca chips, Kerala-style murukku, or a proper raw mango or garlic pickle from back home. That customer exists in Vienna, in your Central European retailer network, and in the South Asian communities your logistics reach daily. Right now, they are buying those products elsewhere, because the category is simply not available through you.

The Minzelis Solution

Minzelis is a Kerala-origin premium ambient food brand, produced in Calicut and positioned specifically to fill this gap in European distribution. The range covers 51 branded snack SKUs, 10 pickles, 12 pulses, and a full pantry extension including chutney powders, breakfast staples, and coconut-based ingredients. Every product is EU-label compliant and ambient, meaning no cold chain, no short shelf life, and no complexity for your existing warehouse and logistics setup at Wien Grossmarkt.

The brands already in your Indian range, HEERA, PATAK, TRS, MDH, do not have a Kerala snack or South Indian pickle category. This is not a gap they have overlooked. It is a structurally different origin and taste profile, one that Minzelis was built to serve. Adding Minzelis does not displace any brand already on your shelves. It opens a category that is currently not available to your customers at all.

Minzelis is managed from Germany and backed by Gramarhein's export-proven infrastructure, actively shipping to 16 countries, giving you an EU-compliant route to capture this category gap with a supplier that already operates at European scale.
