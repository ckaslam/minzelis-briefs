Current Range Analysis

SAE Foods has built its identity around premium-origin, diaspora-relevant branded products from Nepal. With Wai Wai noodles as the flagship and Himalayan coffee as a companion line, the portfolio is cohesive and intentional, anchored by provenance and nostalgia rather than commodity positioning. That same principle, premium origin, emotional resonance, existing diaspora demand, applies with equal force to South Indian and Kerala-origin food in the UK.

The infrastructure SAE Foods has developed to serve UK wholesalers with Nepali branded products is directly transferable to a South Indian range. The customer base overlaps. The distribution logic is identical. The only difference is the origin and the category gap that currently sits unfilled.

The Commercial Gap
The Kerala ambient snack and pickle category represents a significant, daily-consumption segment with no branded import presence to match it. Your wholesaler customers who buy Wai Wai are already serving the same communities that buy Kerala snacks, and right now they have nowhere to source them from a branded, reliable UK importer.
